Quality Produce Since Day One

At Hakimi Fresh Produce, we're dedicated to providing the highest quality fresh fruits and vegetables to Toronto's food industry. With years of experience in the wholesale market, we understand the needs of restaurants, cafés, and stores that demand the freshest ingredients.

Our commitment to quality means we source directly from trusted growers and deliver daily to ensure your produce arrives at peak freshness. Whether you need conventional or organic options, we've got you covered.
